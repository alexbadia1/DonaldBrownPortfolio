EARLY VOTING

Under legislation enacted earlier this year, New York will have early voting for any registered voter on 9 separate dates during the 2-weeks before the general election day on November 5 th . PLEASE NOTE: EARLY VOTING IS LIMITED TO CERTAIN DAYS AND CERTAIN HOURS; AND EARLY VOTES CAN ONLY BE CAST IN ONE OF THE EARLY VOTING SITES DESIGNATED FOR THAT PURPOSE. The dates and the sites are as follows:


Sat. Oct. 26 – Noon – 5 p.m.
Sun. Oct. 27 – Noon – 5 p.m.
Mon. Oct. 28 - 9 a.m. – 5 p.m.
Tues. Oct. 29 -Noon – 8 p.m.
Wed. Oct. 30 – Noon - 8 p.m.

ABSENTEE BALLOTS


              New York permits voting by absentee ballot by persons, who for some valid reason, are unable to get to the polls on election day. Complete information regarding absentee ballots is available on the Dutchess Co. Board of Elections website (www.dutchesselections.com/absenteeballot)   or by calling them at (845) 486-2483, ask for Dep. Commr. Ira Margulies.
              You will find here a sample New York State Absentee Ballot Application and some instructions which you may find helpful in obtaining and filing your absentee ballot.


GETTING TO THE POLLS

              If you did not file an absentee ballot and need transportation assistance in getting to your polling place on election day, please call (845) 380-8522 or me at (845) 849-3590.
